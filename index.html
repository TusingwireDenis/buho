About Buhomart

Buhomart with Passion is a multifaceted enterprise dedicated to promoting African culture, arts, and tourism while supporting local communities through various initiatives.

Our Story

Founded by Ahumuza Justus Joe, Buhomart began as a small crafts shop and has grown into a comprehensive cultural hub that includes an art school, cafeteria, gym, charitable organization, and tour services.

Our mission is to showcase the beauty of African culture while creating sustainable opportunities for local artisans, artists, and communities.

With a passion for preserving traditions and promoting healthy living, we've created a space where culture, creativity, and community come together in Nkwenda-Buhoma Kanungu.
